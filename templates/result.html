Understanding these metrics:
  • Accuracy: The percentage of total predictions that were correct
  • Precision: Of all predicted heart disease cases, how many actually had the disease
  • Recall: Of all actual heart disease cases, how many were correctly identified
  • F1 Score: The harmonic mean of precision and recall, balancing both metrics
Confusion Matrix
What is this?

This matrix shows the counts of true positives (correctly predicted disease), true negatives (correctly predicted healthy), false positives (incorrectly predicted disease), and false negatives (incorrectly predicted healthy).

ROC Curve
What is this?

This curve illustrates the trade-off between sensitivity (true positive rate) and specificity (false positive rate) at various threshold settings. The area under the curve (AUC) measures the model's ability to distinguish between classes.
